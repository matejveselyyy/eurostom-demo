What changed.

The Story section is currently a static two-column layout — eyebrow, headline, body, CTAs, photo. All content shown at once, no scroll choreography. This version preserves every piece of content and the photo, but lets each layer arrive on its own beat.

  • Eyebrow arrives first (700ms) — orients the reader
  • Headline reveals in three lines, sequentially (900ms each, ~70vh of scroll per line)
  • Body paragraphs fade in together with 120ms stagger between them
  • CTAs arrive last — earned, not pre-loaded
  • Photo stays sticky in the right column, scaled 1.06× with subtle Y drift driven by scroll position
  • Stamp ramp from 0.18 → 0.35 opacity as the story plays through
  • Vertical progress ticks on the right edge show what stage you're on

Pin duration: 3 viewport heights (360vh total section). Long enough to feel deliberate, short enough to not overstay.

Mobile: pin disabled, single column, all content shown progressively as it scrolls into normal view.

Reduced motion: pin disabled, all content visible at once, no transitions.
