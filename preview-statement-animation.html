How it works.

The section is height: 420vh with an inner position: sticky stage that pins to the viewport for the duration. As the scroll position passes 20%, 40%, 60%, 80%, 100% of the section, each line transitions through three states: hidden → revealed (active) → muted. The progress ticks up top show which step you're on. The active line gets a tan accent dash to the left and a 12px indent. Tagline closes with the brand voice.

  • Timing: 900ms ease-soft per line transition (matches the rest of the system)
  • Type: Cabinet Grotesk 700 at clamp(2.4rem, 8vw, 6rem), letter-spacing −0.025em
  • Tan dash: 36px line that scales in from left on the active line, mirrors the eyebrow pattern site-wide
  • Reduced motion: all lines visible at once, no transitions
  • Pin duration: ~3.2 viewport scrolls — enough to feel intentional, not so much that it feels stuck

Where it goes: between the Story section and the Services grid. Replaces the first scrolling address marquee. Acts as a bridge from "who we are" to "what we do."

Lines come from real content in the clinic's source — no fabricated copy. The accent words (lékařů, the ellipsis on Vždy s úsměvem) are picked for typographic rhythm, not for marketing emphasis.
